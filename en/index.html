Bed & Breakfast 3 épis

No matter the season, for work or leisure, for a break in nature, for a moment of rest and well-being, let yourself be seduced and discover the Herve Land and in particular the traditional village of Thimister.

It is perfectly situated at the junction of the E40 and E42 highways. It is only about 20 km from : Liège to the west, Aachen to the east, Maastricht to the north and the Circuit of Spa-Francorchamps to the south.

Let yourself experience the joy and tranquility of the countryside...
We will warmly welcome you in this entirely restored 18th century farm, in the middle of the bocages and meadows.

We offer you to discover for breakfast some products of the region, such as the cheese of Herve, the syrup of Liège and the cake of Verviers.
